Article 03 • Dr Paul

QRISK3 Risk Assessment

12%
This person has a 12 in 100 chance of having a heart attack or stroke in the next 10 years

QRISK3 combines multiple factors to give personalised cardiovascular risk estimates

Key Risk Factors in QRISK3

📊

Age & Gender

Risk increases with age, men generally higher risk than women

❤️

Blood Pressure

Both systolic and diastolic pressures factored into calculation

🩸

Cholesterol Ratio

Total cholesterol divided by HDL cholesterol level

🚬

Smoking Status

Current smoking significantly increases calculated risk

⚖️

BMI

Body mass index reflects obesity-related cardiovascular risk

🧬

Medical History

Diabetes, kidney disease, family history, other conditions

Treatment Thresholds

<10%
Low Risk
Lifestyle advice, reassurance, regular monitoring
10%+
Consider Statins
Discuss benefits and risks of medication with patient
20%+
High Risk
Strong recommendation for statin therapy plus lifestyle changes

What the Research Shows

Statin Effectiveness

22% per mmol

Each 1 mmol/L LDL reduction with statins reduces major events by 22%

Lifestyle Impact

40-50% reduction

Key Takeaways

• QRISK3 provides personalised 10-year cardiovascular risk estimates

• Treatment decisions based on calculated risk, not single risk factors

• 10% risk threshold commonly used for statin consideration

• Risk-based approach proven to prevent cardiovascular events
