FODMAPs — What to Trial-Exclude & What to Eat Instead
Restrict high-FODMAP foods for 2–6 weeks · Then reintroduce one category at a time to find your triggers · Ideally with a registered dietitian
Category
🔴 High FODMAP — trial-exclude
🟢 Low FODMAP — well tolerated
🥛 Dairy
Cow's milk, yoghurt, soft cheese, ice cream, custard
Lactose-free milk, hard cheese (cheddar, parmesan), butter, brie, camembert
🍞 Grains
Wheat bread, rye, barley, wheat pasta, most breakfast cereals, couscous
Rice, oats, quinoa, gluten-free bread & pasta, corn, polenta, buckwheat
🧅 Vegetables
Onion, garlic, leek, asparagus, cauliflower, mushrooms, artichoke
Carrot, tomato, courgette, spinach, potato, pepper, cucumber, green beans
🍎 Fruit
Apple, pear, mango, watermelon, cherry, peach, dried fruit, fruit juice
Firm banana, strawberry, blueberry, orange, kiwi, grape, pineapple
🫘 Legumes
Baked beans, red lentils, chickpeas, kidney beans, split peas
Canned rinsed chickpeas (small portion), firm tofu, tempeh, edamame (small)
🍯 Sweeteners
Honey, sorbitol, mannitol, xylitol, fructose, high-fructose corn syrup
White or brown sugar (small amount), maple syrup (small), glucose, stevia
This is a diagnostic tool, not a permanent diet. Most people can reintroduce the majority of foods once individual triggers are identified.
