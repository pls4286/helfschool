Article 01 • Dr Paul

Understanding Blood Pressure

140 / 90 mmHg
Systolic (140)
Pressure when heart beats
Diastolic (90)
Pressure between beats

140/90 mmHg or above = hypertension diagnosis

Why Does Hypertension Matter?

1 in 4
UK adults have high blood pressure
NHS Digital
50%
Don't know they have it
British Heart Foundation
70,000
Deaths per year in UK
ONS • 2021

Often called the "silent killer" because most people have no symptoms until serious complications develop

Treatment Approach

1

Lifestyle Changes

Reduce salt, lose weight, exercise regularly, limit alcohol

2

Single Medication

ACE inhibitor, calcium channel blocker, or thiazide diuretic

3

Dual Therapy

Combination of two medications if single drug insufficient

4

Triple Therapy

Three medications + specialist referral if needed

What the Research Shows

Blood Pressure Reduction

10 mmHg reduction

Reduces risk of heart disease by 20% and stroke by 27%

Lancet • 2016 meta-analysis

Lifestyle Interventions

5-10 mmHg drop

Weight loss and salt reduction alone can achieve significant reductions

Cochrane Review • 2020

SPRINT Trial

25% reduction

Lower BP targets (120 vs 140) reduced cardiovascular events

NEJM • 2015

24-Hour Monitoring

Better outcomes

Ambulatory monitoring more accurate than clinic readings

BMJ • 2019

Key Takeaways

• Hypertension affects 1 in 4 UK adults — half don't know they have it

• Often symptomless until serious complications develop

• Lifestyle changes can achieve significant blood pressure reductions

• Effective medications available with proven cardiovascular benefits
