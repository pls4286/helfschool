The Mediterranean Diet

PREDIMED trial · ~30% reduction in major cardiovascular events

Vegetables & fruit

The foundation — at every meal, in abundance

Whole grains

Bread, pasta, rice — unrefined where possible

Fish

Especially oily fish — at least twice a week

Legumes

Lentils, chickpeas, beans — high fibre, low glycaemic

Olive oil & nuts

The key fat sources — used generously in place of butter
