Step 1 of 3
TODAY'S CHANGE
Butter
→
Extra-virgin olive oil
Swap butter for extra-virgin olive oil
Use it for cooking, drizzling, and dressings. This single swap shifts your fat intake from saturated to monounsaturated — the most important fat in the Mediterranean diet.
Step 2 of 3
THIS WEEK'S CHANGE
🐟
Add oily fish twice this week
Salmon, mackerel, sardines, herring, or anchovies. Tinned is completely fine — and affordable. Two portions a week is the Mediterranean target, and the research shows it makes a real difference to cardiovascular risk.
Tinned sardines on toast Salmon fillet with vegetables Mackerel with salad Anchovies in pasta
Step 3 of 3
THIS MONTH'S CHANGE
🥦
Make vegetables the majority of every plate
Not a side dish — the centre of the meal. Aim for half your plate or more. More colour means more variety of protective compounds. Add a tin of chickpeas to stews, spinach to pasta, roast a tray of vegetables alongside everything.
Roasted Mediterranean veg Tinned chickpeas in stew Spinach into any pasta Big salad as the main
