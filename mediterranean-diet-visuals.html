Three tiers — what to eat freely, regularly, and occasionally
🌿
EVERY DAY — at every meal
Vegetables · fruit · whole grains · legumes · olive oil — the bulk of what you eat
🥦 Vegetables — all kinds, in abundance 🍎 Fruit — 2–3 portions daily 🌾 Whole grains 🫘 Lentils & chickpeas 🥜 Nuts & seeds 🫒 Extra-virgin olive oil 💧 Water as main drink
🐟
SEVERAL TIMES A WEEK
Fish · eggs · poultry · dairy — important, but not at every meal
🐟 Oily fish (salmon, mackerel, sardines, herring) — 2+ times/week 🥚 Eggs — up to 4/week 🍗 Poultry 🧀 Yogurt & cheese (moderate)
⚠️
OCCASIONAL — not daily
Red meat · processed food · added sugar — not forbidden, just rare
🥩 Red meat 🥓 Processed meats 🍰 Added sugar 🍞 Refined white grains 🛒 Ultra-processed foods
The PREDIMED trial — the strongest dietary evidence we have
~30%
↓ reduction in heart attacks, strokes & cardiovascular death
vs a standard low-fat control diet · PREDIMED · New England Journal of Medicine 2018 re-analysis

What was studied

👥
7,447 participants at high cardiovascular risk in Spain — none had established heart disease at the start
🎲
Randomly assigned to: Mediterranean + extra-virgin olive oil · Mediterranean + mixed nuts · or low-fat control
⏱️
Stopped after 4.8 years — the monitoring board halted it early because the cardiovascular benefit was clear
📊
Hazard ratio 0.70 (95% CI 0.54–0.92) — HR 0.70 = 30% lower risk vs the control group ⚑

The evidence goes well beyond the heart
❤️
Heart disease & stroke
Lower risk of major cardiovascular events in people at high baseline risk — the strongest dietary evidence in prevention medicine.
PREDIMED · NEJM 2018 re-analysis
↓ Risk ~30%
🩺
Type 2 diabetes
Lower risk of developing type 2 diabetes at higher adherence — consistent across 10 studies and 136,846 participants.
Koloverou et al. · Metabolism · 2014 meta-analysis
↓ Risk 23%
🧠
Dementia & brain health
Lower risk of Alzheimer's disease and cognitive decline — growing evidence from a 2025 meta-analysis covering 24 years of studies.
Dominguez LJ et al. · GeroScience · 2025
↓ Risk ~30%
What the evidence shows
Start with one meal.
Not a transformation.

🥦
