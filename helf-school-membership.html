Frequently asked questions

Can I cancel my membership at any time?

Yes, absolutely — no questions asked. You can cancel your monthly or annual membership at any time from your account settings. You'll retain access until the end of your billing period.

Is this medical advice?

No. helf.school is a health education platform. Everything here is written to help you understand health topics — not to diagnose, treat, or replace the advice of your doctor. Always consult a healthcare professional about your personal health.

How does the annual plan work?

The annual plan is billed as a single payment of £60 — saving you £12 compared to paying monthly. It renews automatically each year unless you cancel beforehand.

Is my payment secure?

Yes. All payments are processed securely through Stripe or PayPal — two of the world's most trusted payment platforms. helf.school never stores your card details.

What's included in the free trial?

Your 14-day free trial gives you full Member access — every article, every research summary, and full community forum participation. If you decide it's not for you, simply cancel before the trial ends and you won't be charged.

What is the Lifetime membership?

A one-off payment of £120 gives you permanent Member access — no recurring charges, ever. It's the best value option for anyone who intends to use helf.school long-term, and helps support the platform's future.

📋 Important: helf.school is a health education platform. Nothing here constitutes medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.
