Can I cancel at any time?

Yes — monthly and annual memberships can be cancelled at any time. You keep access until the end of the billing period.

What's the difference between monthly and annual?

Same full access either way. Annual works out at £5/month instead of £6 — saving you £12 over the year.

Is this medical advice?

No. helf.school is health education — not medical advice. Always speak to your own doctor about anything that feels personally relevant.

What does Lifetime access include?

Everything in Member, forever — including all future content as it's published. One payment, no further charges.

How often is new content added?

New condition guides and research analyses are added regularly. Members are notified first.

Who writes the content?

All content is written by Dr Paul — a retired NHS GP with over 20 years of clinical experience. Every claim links to a peer-reviewed source.
